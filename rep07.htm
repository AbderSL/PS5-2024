Safety Guide

Learn how to use your PS5 console safely with the supplied Safety Guide. This printed manual also contains product warranty terms and information about specifications. Be sure to read it before using your console.

User's Guide

Find out about everything your PS5 console can do. Learn how to configure settings and how to use each function. From your PS5 console's home screen, go to Settings  > User's Guide, Health & Safety, and Other Information > User's Guide.
